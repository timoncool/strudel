## Blue Monday — New Order

Культовый синтипоп трек.

<MiniRepl
  client:visible
  tune={`// "Blue Monday" - New Order style
stack(
  s("bd bd [bd*4] bd").slow(4),
  s("~ hh").gain(0.5),
  n("<[2 ~ 2 ~ 2 3] [3 ~ 3 ~ 3 3]>")
    .slow(2).scale("d2:minor")
    .s("sawtooth").lpf(800).gain(0.6)
).cpm(130)`}
/>
